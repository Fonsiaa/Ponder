Wind - Did you know

Wind is the movement of air near Earth’s surface. It can be a gentle breeze or a strong gale. Changes in temperature cause wind. When air flows over a warm surface, it heats up and rises.

Rain - Did you know

Rain is an important part of the water cycle. Rain falls from clouds in the sky in the form of water droplets, this is called precipitation.

Cloud - Did you know

There are a range of different types of clouds, the main types include stratus, cumulus, and cirrus. Stratus clouds are flat and featureless, appearing as layered sheets. Cumulus clouds are puffy, like cotton floating in the sky. Cirrus clouds are thin and wispy, appearing high in the sky.
